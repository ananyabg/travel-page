Welcome to my Travel Book

The travel book is a recollection of memories & stories that were part of various adventures.

Ice & Sun

The sun doesn't always melt the ice. It gives a golden glow on the white surface giving it a spectacular look especially during sunrise. Kanchenjunga is one of the best examples of it. When the early rays of the sun falls on the snowcapped peaks, the view looks astounding.

Whale Story

The sky is blue and so is the deep sea making it a wonderful contrast of the shades of the same color. A deep dive into the sea & whale invites you to visit his kingdom decorated with coral reefs & colorful fishes.Enigmatic..

The Block

When the pandemic strikes and you need to be at home & stay safe. This would be a temprary halt like this translucent ice block.It would slowly melt down in days to come paving the way for new adventures again.
